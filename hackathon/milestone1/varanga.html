Vanranga Jain Temple

The Vanranga Jain Temple, situated in the serene town of Vanranga, is an important pilgrimage site for the Jain community in Mangaluru. Known for its tranquil environment and exquisite architecture, this temple holds significant religious and cultural value.

Dedicated to Lord Parshwanatha, the 23rd Tirthankara, the temple is a center for worship and spiritual practices. The intricate carvings on the temple's walls and pillars showcase exceptional craftsmanship, representing various Jain motifs and symbols.

The temple complex includes a beautifully adorned main shrine, which houses the idol of Lord Parshwanatha. The idol is made of white marble and is meticulously sculpted, reflecting the high standards of Jain artistic traditions.

Surrounding the main shrine are several smaller shrines dedicated to other Tirthankaras and revered Jain saints. The temple grounds are adorned with lush gardens and peaceful meditation areas, providing a serene space for visitors to reflect and pray.
