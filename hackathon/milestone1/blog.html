Coastal Karnataka Diary - Sojourn of Mangalore and Around

November 20, 2019

What is the first thought that comes to mind when we talk about the coastal town of Mangalore? For me, since my first visit to Udupi, I was in love with the beaches and vegetarian South Indian food. These were the two things topmost in my mind when I set out for my second city sojourn in coastal Karnataka - Mangalore. Though being much larger in size compared to Udupi, it wasn't easy to travel the length and breadth of the city in a way I love to travel - in public transport and still be able to tick all the attractions of the town off my bucket list. With limited holidays in hand, I set out on my solo trip to Mangalore and the nearby holy city of Dharmasthala.
